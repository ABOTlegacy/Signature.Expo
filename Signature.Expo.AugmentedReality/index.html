Treasure Hunting

Goal

You are an eager treasure hunter searching for the Big Reward, the Signature QR Code! Along you're hunt, you will find other reward goodies about the WennSoft Signature Team.

How To Play

Play by using the table (Treasure Detector). By utilizing the rear camera, you will be hunting the different AR Codes for hidden treasure. When the Treasure Detector starts beeping, click the dig for treasure action.

Final Notes

We hope you enjoy the hunt, (you might even learn something along the way.) Good Luck!
